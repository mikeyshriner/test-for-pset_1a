BLACKPINK Jennie

Kim Jennie

Kim Jennie is better known as JENNIE and is the main rapper of girl group BLACKPINK. She was born in Seoul, South Korea on January 16, 1996 but moved to New Zealand for studies. She moved back to Korea after finding YG Entertainment at age 14. She is currently 23 years old.
