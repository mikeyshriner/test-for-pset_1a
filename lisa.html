Lalisa Manoban

Lalisa Manoban is better known as LISA and is the youngest member and main dancer of the girl group BLACKPINK. She is the only member without Korean descent. She was born and raised in the Buriram Province in Thailand and moved to South Korea to persue a career with YG Entertainment when she was only 13. She was born on March 27, 1997 and is currently 22 years old.
