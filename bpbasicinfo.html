Basic Information

History

Blackpink was first announced as a group under YG Entertainment on June 29, 2016. The group contains members Jennie, Jisoo, Rose, and Lisa. They released their first two singles, BOOMBAYAH and WHISTLE on August 8, 2018. WHISTLE went number 1 in South Korea and BOOMBAYAH went number 1 on the US World Chart. Their next two singles were Playing With Fire and STAY which were released on November 1 of the same year. Their final single before their temporary music hiatus was As If Its Your Last which was released on June 22, 2017. During their year music hiatus, they released their YouTube series called BLACKPINK House. After their year long hiatus, they came back on June 15, 2018 with their biggest hit to date DDU DU DDU DU. The song peaked at number 1 in South Korea and was also the first song to chart on the Billboard Hot 100 by an Asian girl group. Their most recent single, Kill This Love was released on April 4, 2019.
