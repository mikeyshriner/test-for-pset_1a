Park Chaeyoung

Park Chaeyoung is better known as ROSE and is the main vocalist of girl group BLACKPINK. She was born on February 11, 1997 in Aukland New Zealand and raised in Melbourne, Australia. She moved to South Korea, which is the country she descends from, at age 15 to persue a music career. She is currently 22 years old
